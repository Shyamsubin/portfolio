Facebook

A social networking platform for connecting with friends, family, and communities.

Instagram

Instagram is a social media platform owned by Meta Platforms (formerly Facebook). It primarily focuses on photo and video sharing, allowing users to upload, edit, and share visual content with their followers. Users can also interact with other users' posts through likes, comments, and shares.

Whatsapp

WhatsApp is a free, globally available messaging and calling app owned by Meta. It allows users to send text and voice messages, make voice and video calls, and share various media like photos, videos, and documents. WhatsApp is known for its end-to-end encryption, ensuring privacy for user communications.

Metaverse

The metaverse is a digital environment that mimics the real world, allowing users to interact with each other and digital content through avatars. It's a persistent, shared, and immersive virtual space that combines virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR), and other technologies to create a sense of presence and interaction.
